Welcome to Tropical Treats Bakery of Australia

Bragging on my new bakery, great location near Willows Shopping Centre. I am an artisan-baker and I trained in Paris. Some of my current customers are James Cook University, the Cowboys and Telstra.

Based in Queensland, Adelaide, Perth, the Tropical Treats Bakery provides one of the best bakery products throughout the year, in a diverse range of menus including cakes, muffins, macaroons, breads, pie's and biscuits.
