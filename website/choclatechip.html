Chocolate Chip Muffin

A Chocolate Chip muffin is an individual-sized, baked product. It can refer to two distinct items, a part-raised flatbread (typically unsweetened) and a cupcake-like quickbread (often sweetened). The flatbread is of British or European derivation, and dates from at least the early 18th century, while the quickbread originated in North America during the 19th century. Both are common worldwide today.
